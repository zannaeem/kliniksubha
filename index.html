ABOUT KLINIK SUBHA

Redefining Healthcare Excellence.

Klinik Subha, established in 1990 and operating under Jash Pillay Medresources Sdn Bhd since 2018, is a trusted healthcare provider in Beaufort, Sabah. Renowned for comprehensive medical services and specialized physiotherapy care, we offer a wide range of treatments, including general practice, pain management, and diagnostic services.

With a team of three experienced medical officers and one physiotherapist, we are committed to delivering high-quality, holistic, and affordable care to our patients. Our modern, well-equipped facility features advanced diagnostic tools like ultrasound, X-ray, ECG, and blood count machines, ensuring precise and efficient care.

Conveniently located at Beaufort Square and open daily from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Klinik Subha strives to make healthcare accessible to everyone, setting new standards in patient-centered care.
