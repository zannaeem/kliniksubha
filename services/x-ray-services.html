Our Services

X-Ray Services

Our advanced X-ray imaging services provide detailed diagnostic information to help identify fractures, infections, tumors, and other medical conditions. We use modern digital X-ray equipment for high-quality images with minimal radiation exposure.

Available X-Ray Examinations
  • Chest X-Ray: Lungs, heart, ribs, and chest wall
  • Bone X-Ray: Fractures, joint problems, bone infections
  • Spine X-Ray: Neck, back, and spinal column
  • Skull X-Ray: Head injuries and skull abnormalities
  • Abdominal X-Ray: Digestive system and abdominal organs
  • Dental X-Ray: Teeth and jaw examinations
Why Choose Our X-Ray Services?
  • Digital X-ray technology for better image quality
  • Minimal radiation exposure for patient safety
  • Experienced radiographers and technicians
  • Fast results and digital image storage
  • Comprehensive reporting by qualified radiologists
  • Affordable pricing for all examinations
